GUARD: Halt, traveler. The outpost gates stay sealed until the Warden returns from the northern watch.
PLAYER: And when will that be?
GUARD: Soon enough. Though — between the two of us — the Warden fell three nights ago. The elders simply haven't said so aloud yet.
PLAYER: Then who holds the garrison?
GUARD: Commander Vale, of course. She's led it since the siege, three winters back.
PLAYER: I'll wait for the elders, then.
ELDER: Be at ease, traveler. The Warden lives and is in good health; she will address the outpost herself at dusk. All is well within these walls.
